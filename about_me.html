My name is Brandon Apol. I was born to military parents in 1994 and have ever since been a lover of travel and adventure. After graduating from university in Dayton, Ohio, I moved to Alaska to teach music in public elementary schools, which has allowed me to move about the state. I met my wife, Lydia, at a church event in South Carolina ten years ago and forgot about her completely until about three years ago, when we reconnected and one date led to another.

We currently live in Fairbanks, Alaska, where I enjoy a litany of activities. A few of them include:
- Outdoor things: Rock climbing, hiking, mountaineering, cross-country skiing, ice climbing, photography, fishing, berry picking, kayaking, general exploring
- Indoor things: I am a music teacher (I play with egg shakers with little kids), and I play a lot of guitar and drums as well as some piano and bass. We own an old Nintendo 64 and my wife and I play Legend of Zelda, Smash Brothers etc. I cook. I read a lot. My wife works at a co-op grocery store and brings home some of the best fruit in Alaska. We are slowly collecting a mass of local art.
- Social events: Fairbanks has a slew of breweries, two rock climbing gyms, but somehow only two independent coffee shops, which I regularly attend.

I would like to be a software programmer because I like the lifestyle it can give me. I want to be able to have a large family and to be able to enjoy spending time with them. Working remotely as a programmer means I can be in proximity to them and live on a single income. It also allows for travel so we can visit family in South Carolina while also enabling us to live in Alaska.
